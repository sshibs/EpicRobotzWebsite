Instructions for Printing Stickers

These stickers are suitable for labeling
were badges are to be hung or stored.  Their
raw form is about the same size of a badge but
without the barcode.  To print them:

1. Download the sticker to your PC.

2. Use Word to print them.  Start a new, blank
document in Word.  

3. Drag and drop the image onto the docuemnt.

4. To keep the original size of the image, right 
click on it, and select "Size and Position".  The
image is 590x712 pixels, or about 2x2.4 inches.

5. Adjust the size and postion according to your
paper, and print.
